Weekend Services

Address 303 West Maple Street, Nicholasville, KY 40356
Service Times Sunday at 9:30 AM (Adult Sunday School), 10:45 AM (Main Sanctuary)

NMC is located in the heart of downtown Nicholasville, just two blocks behind the Jessamine County Courthouse.

We strive to be a welcoming home to every person who comes through our doors; our staff and volunteers would love to talk with you and answer any questions! It’s a good idea to arrive at least 15 minutes early to visit with your church family!

If you or a family member has a disability and wants to get connected, please contact us. We would love to support you!

We try to structure parking in a way that allows the maximum number of people to attend our services. If you are a first-time visitor, we have special parking spots available!

As far as what to wear, you’re invited to come as you are, whether it’s a suit or shorts you feel most comfortable wearing. We tend to dress pretty casual.
