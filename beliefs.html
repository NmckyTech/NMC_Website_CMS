Our Beliefs?

At Nicholasville Methodist Church, we believe in the transformative power of faith and the teachings of Jesus Christ. Our core beliefs are rooted in the principles of love, grace, and community. We are committed to fostering a welcoming environment where individuals can grow spiritually, serve others, and make a positive impact in the world. We uphold the doctrines of the United Methodist Church, emphasizing the importance of scripture, tradition, reason, and experience in our faith journey. Join us as we seek to live out our faith through worship, fellowship, and service.
