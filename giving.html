Why We Give

We desire to be generous, because generosity is the way of those who call Christ their Lord. We believe generosity is a distinct trait of believers in Christ, and Scripture points to this! Our hope at NMC is that through our generous giving, we show what Christ is like to all the world.

⁶ “The person who sows sparingly will also reap sparingly, and the person who sows generously will also reap generously. ⁷ Each person should do as he has decided in his heart—not reluctantly or out of compulsion, since God loves a cheerful giver.” 2 CORINTHIANS 9: 6-7

Methods of Giving

Here are a few easy ways you can support the mission of Nicholasville Methodist Church.

Currently, you can give to:

  • →General Fund, which supports the majority of our ministry activities.
  • →Missions & Missions Fund, which supports our church's missions program and global missions activities.
  • →Building Fund, which supports our building expansion projects.

If you have any questions or want to talk more about your giving, our finance team would love to help. Just reach out to us at finance@nmcky.org. We would love to speak with you!

You can give by credit card, debit card, or directly from your bank account. Please consider giving directly from your bank account because the processing fees are minimal and your gift will go further.

Our Giving Accountability

Knowing that God wants us to be faithful stewards of the generous gifts we receive, we prioritize accountability and transparency in our finances.

Our leadership reviews monthly financial statements to direct the mission of our church, and our finances are audited annually by an independent third-party. Four years of audited financials are posted on our website.
